River Rush Adventure

Dive into a thrilling 2-day white water rafting experience down the majestic Rapids River. This trip is perfect for adrenaline seekers and nature lovers. You'll encounter class III and IV rapids, enjoy overnight camping by the riverbank, and explore scenic canyons with experienced guides ensuring your safety and fun.

Family Float Experience

A calm and relaxing trip ideal for families with kids or beginners. This half-day float along the Lazy Stream features gentle currents, wildlife sightings, and a riverside picnic. Our certified guides provide safety gear and fun facts along the way. A safe and memorable way to enjoy nature with your loved ones.

Sunset Canyon Expedition

Join us for a magical evening paddle through Sunset Canyon. This 4-hour guided trip begins in the late afternoon and ends just as the sun sets over the cliffs. Expect glowing canyon walls, photo opportunities, and a peaceful experience. Snacks and headlamps are provided for a serene return journey.
